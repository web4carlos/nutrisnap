# NutriSnap Vision

NutriSnap is not another calorie counter. It is an intelligent personal nutrition platform.

## Product principles

1. Nutrition guidance should be understandable.
2. Logging food should be fast.
3. AI should be integrated throughout the product.
4. Recommendations should explain their reasoning.
5. The experience should feel premium in English and Spanish.
6. Each release must leave the application usable.
